Kryption

Kryption lets you encrypt and decrypt a message using a secure formula including a passphrase. The passphrase and can be as short or long as you want. The shorter the passphrase however, the less secure the encryption is. While you shouldn't be able to crack the encryption, we have no liability if your code is broken.
